PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE

Pamela Gomes is a Technical Support professional with over 3 years of experience in corporate and multinational environments, working at both Level 1 and Level 2. She is fluent in English, Spanish, and Portuguese, and has supported users from over 80 nationalities. She has hands-on experience supporting internal and SaaS applications, managing performance metrics, building knowledge bases, and resolving incidents related to automation and access control.

Pamela handles daily technical communications with clients and manages multiple ongoing cases, with a strong focus on accurate ticket categorization, root cause analysis, and collaboration with development, product, and operations teams. In addition to her technical background, Pamela has led international teams and provided academic mentoring in volunteer and educational settings. She coordinated language tutoring programs, monitored team performance, and implemented support and engagement strategies.

Pamela is currently focused on transitioning into software development. She is actively studying programming and expanding her technical skills with the goal of becoming a software developer and contributing to the development of robust, scalable applications.
